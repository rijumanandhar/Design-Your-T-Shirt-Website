How This Webite Was Made Page 2

The fifth page is the Login and Sign up Page. The div #container of this page has two sections. One section is for login form and the other is for sign up form. Login form asks for Email and Password. Sign up form asks for other information including name, email, password, etc. With JavaScript, the user is alerted if any text field is empty or if the passwords don’t match.

The last page is about how this website is made. The div #container of this page gives us information about how this website was built.
